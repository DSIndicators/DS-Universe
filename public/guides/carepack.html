DS Checklist

DS Checklist puts your trading plan where your eyes already are. Write your own entry rules into a clean panel, then tick them off one by one before you ever click buy or sell. The Math, As You Drag — 03

DS Risk-Reward

Click your entry, drag to your stop, and DS Risk-Reward draws the whole trade for you — a red risk zone below and stacked green target rails above at 1R, 2R, 3R, each labelled in points and dollars. Size it by contracts and the numbers update live, so you see exactly what you're risking and what you stand to make before you commit.
